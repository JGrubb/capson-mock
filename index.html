CapsonCare – A New Standard of Protection

How CapsonCare Works - See it in Action

Unnecessary claims and lawsuits are filed against physicians everyday as a result of patients not feeling heard. We hear again and again from physicians that they are distracted by the threat, financial risk and emotional impact of litigation. We also hear from patients who are not only frustrated, but feel intimidated, alone and ignored. We believe that by facilitating dialog between our members and patients, that some conflicts can be prevented in the first place. As a response, we’ve created a new standard of protection through our conflict prevention program.

Why CapsonCare

  • Strengthens patient-doctor relationship by improving communication
  • Drives higher patient loyalty and strengthens your reputation
  • Helps prevent conflict from occurring in the first place
  • Differentiates your practice
  • Provides you with rich, valuable feedback to improve your practice
  • Actively engages patients in the feedback process, improving loyalty and supporting referrals

How CapsonCare Works:

  • CapsonCare solicits honest feedback— positive and negative—from an iPad based survey that patients complete prior to leaving your office
  • The process for your patients – and your office – is simple; we provide you with a Capson iPad, which your patients use to complete a very brief survey after each appointment
  • CapsonCare processes responses in real-time and shares the data with you on a weekly basis
  • CapsonCare is available with all Capson policies; but if you’re not covered by Capson, you can still buy CapsonCare
